INTRODUCTION TO MACHINE LEARNING

Definition 1. Its a Subset of AI 2. Its like teaching a computer to learn from data and make decisions( just like humans learn from experience) Why Machine Learning is necessary: 1. To handle the huge amounts of data 2. Real time predictions 3. Better Decision Making 3. To Automate decisions 4. To deal with complex problems that are too complex for humans to code. 5. Used for high personalized experiences Uses Cases & Real life Scenarios: 1. Industry Applications eg: Healthcare , Finance and Entertainment 2.Education eg : Duolingo app 3. Stock market eg : Stock Predictions 4. Transportation eg: Traffic prediction ML Vs Generative AI 1. ML- Learns patterns from data to predict outcomes eg : predicting the house price 2. Gen AI - Learn patterns from data to generate new content eg: creating a house design Types of Machine Learning: 1. Supervised Learning - this model learns data from labeled data (inputs with known answers)- Predicts outcomes for new and unseen data eg : spam detection 2. Unsupervised Learning - this model learns from unlabeled data - no answers given - discovering the relationships in data eg: market based analysis 3. Reinforcement Learning - this model learns from trial and error method - either they receive rewards or penalties - Optimizing the decisions eg: Self driving cars
